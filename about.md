## about me

I'm an undergraduate student at the University of Virginia studying Computer Science and Mathematics. I work as both a researcher and an engineer, working across software engineering, machine learning, and modeling and simulation.

I like problems where I can engage with both the science (why/how) and the system (what), which motivates what I do.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy playing basketball, lifting, trying new food, and listening to a wide range of music. I also like traveling when I can and learning more about the world.

I'm open to research collaborations, interesting engineering problems, or just a conversation. Feel free to reach out.
